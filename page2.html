These are custom, on-demand, Maps-as-a-Service™ products

First target market : Last Mile Logisitics

There are increasingly more delivery robot startup companies popping up all over the world. Already there is pushback from city planners, and inhabitants as there is a challenge to find space for these amazing innovations..  3DCityIO is set to define where robots and humans should share space - in what we refer to as creating virtual 'robot corridors', or logistics paths, that play well with the humans..

Starting with mobility [e..g. last mile logisitics], we aim to provide any robotics company with a custom service and product, delivered in the form of an [XD Map™]. Under certain agreements, this may be shared on a future, publicly accessible resource called [City A.I™].  This City AI will help visualize and input these map trajectories, and find that optimal path for delivery.  These efforts are simply focused on improving their delivery services, but with partnering closely with robot manufacturers and cities, we can help create that consortium necessary to help the entire industry move forward in a responible and expeditious way., help them expand their particular reach, and improve the quality of their trajectory - thereby helping to optimize their service corridors.

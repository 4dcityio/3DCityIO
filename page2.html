The evolution of city planning - building intelligent cities.

Why not use the term 'smart cities' ?  Because we hate buzz words... although true - not really. We switched only temporarily in fact, so that we can reserve that gradiose term for the end goal when we finally achieve general A.I. to help manage cities - still many decades out. For now, we strive to bring choas into order, with a simple metric giving back time to the city inhabitant. Giving back time, is what we consider as the hallmark success metric for any good robotics innovation.

Proper City Planning is the foundation of a collaborative and vibrant city culture.  City planning should shift from being centered around productivty, but rather to factor in the general well being of city inhabitants, as well.

Exponential population growth and unfettered city expansion has led to the fragmentation of our city and mega cities. Historically, city planning has been centered around the personal vehicle, and unfortunatley the tools for simulation are still not part of city planning discussions.

At 3DCityIO we strongly believe that we can accelerate Robotics & A.I. via readily accesible HD maps.  Company partnerships will help us start solving these cumulative problems, and give us that necessary 'pulse' and 'city vitals' to measure success in design i.e. happiness of the citizen.  We are starting like most, with mobility - but with a twist.

3DCityIO will readily produce on-demand 3D maps with custom overlays,  Maps-as-a-Service that is accesible to anyone, at a reasonable cost. We aim to provide a central repository of high-definition 3D/4D/XD mapping data so that large simulations can collaborate.  All is easily accessed for use in AR/VR, simulations, SLAM, IoT innovations and more.  The idea being that in general, contextual data overlays and ground truth are essential to progress in robotics - hence, should be accesible..
